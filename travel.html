Places I've Been

Paris, France

The City of Light is one of my favorite destinations. The Eiffel Tower, the Louvre, and the charming streets make it unforgettable.

Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo is a vibrant city blending tradition and modernity. The food, culture, and technology are amazing!

New York City, USA

The Big Apple is always buzzing with energy. Times Square, Central Park, and Broadway are must-see attractions.
